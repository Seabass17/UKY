UKY Premiership

Final table — current season

Season story: Portmaris Vikings won the Premiership by a single point from Grindalythe Monarchs — despite winning four fewer games. The Vikings' relentless try-scoring rugby earned them 20 bonus points across the season. The title was settled in the final fixture at the YNS Arena: Vikings 34–29 Monarchs. The Monarchs needed the Vikings not to score a fifth try. The Vikings scored it in the 79th minute. Thorham Rams finished third in the surprise of the season, earning their first ever Stonemark Continental Shield place. SASA returned to the Premiership and finished a creditable 10th. Aldgate Foresters, the other promoted side, face the playoff after a difficult debut campaign.
